The Ultimate Pub Quiz

Instructions

  1. On the main screen, click Play Game to get started (use the back button below to return)

  2. Choose a difficulty

  3. Read the question carefully and select the correct answer. There is only one correct answer for each question

  4. At the end of the quiz, you will be presented with your final score

  5. Want to enter the Hall of Fame? Simply add your name to the 'Enter Hall of Fame' section and click 'Enter'
